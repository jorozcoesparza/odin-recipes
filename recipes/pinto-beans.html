Pinto Beans

Description

A an easy and simple stew to brew

Ingredients

  • Two cups of pinto beans
  • Three garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Optional garnish: tomato, lime and cheese

Steps

  1. Soak two cups of dried pinto beans overnight
  2. Drain and rinse beans
  3. Transfer beans onto a pot and add six cups of water
  4. Boil for two hours, checking periodically until beans are tender
  5. Once tender, add salt to taste
  6. Garnish with dice tomato, a splash of lime, and cheese
