Salad

Description

A simple yet flavorful salad recipe

Ingredients

  • A head of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Caesar dressing
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut lettuce into bite-size chunks
  2. Mix lettuce and dressing in a bown
  3. Garnish with croutons and parmesan cheese
  4. Drizzle with olive oil
  5. Optional: add salt and pepper to taste
